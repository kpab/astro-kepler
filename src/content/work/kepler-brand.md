## Overview

Kepler needed an identity that felt confident and a little playful without
tipping into gimmick. The answer was a disciplined system: two hues, one
geometric motif, and a signature shadow.

## The system

- **Two hues only** — navy for structure, orange for energy
- **Orbital mark** — a ring and a dot, endlessly reusable
- **Hard-offset shadow** — a solid block, never a blur

## Approach

Every asset derives from the same tokens, so the brand scales from a favicon to a
billboard without redrawing anything. Constraints did the heavy lifting.

## Outcome

A living design system that non-designers can extend safely, because the rules
are few enough to remember.
